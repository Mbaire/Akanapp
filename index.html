Ghananian Akan Names

Here is a short description of what the Akan Names entail.

Ghananians have a unique way of naming their children which is based on the day that a child is born

In many parts of Ghana, and elsewhere in West Africa, a person’s name is determined by the day of the week on which she/he was born.
